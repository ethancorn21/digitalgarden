The way I see it, I have to play the game in order to get where I want to be to exert the influence I need to achieve my goals. 

Now, this sounds extremely nefarious and perhaps I sound like a psychopath. But there is a certain amount of influence, status, and power that I need to attain to project my ideas into reality. Ideas which I believe could make the world a better place. 

In order to get this level of prestige, I have to play the invisible game. The invisible game we all play. Whether we are being nice to our coworkers who we despise or follow the laws of the road. We are playing the game. The game that keeps us, as a society, from waking up and returning to our primordial propensity to inflict extreme acts of violence while only wearing our birthday suits.

Within the societal rules exists a game. A game some people play by being gang bangers and gun-toters, while others play it as the narcissistic banker who frauds grandmas out of their retirement checks.

Either way, like a game they chose their roles. I have chosen mine, and like an RPG of sorts, I have skills and side quests that need to be maxed out in order for me to unlock parts of the main mission. My life's mission. So yes, I will play the game. I will follow the laws of the road and drive like a lawful civilian. But I will also bend and break those which I don't like. Like everyone, I am an NPC.
