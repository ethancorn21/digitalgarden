# generation effect
Information that is put into your own words and explanations are better remembered than ones that are passively learned. Active learning > passive learning. Active learning looks like *engaging* with the information you have received. It can look like , as I mentioned above, putting words into your own words, or connecting it to already known ideas / concepts.
### example:
When your read a book, put what you want to remember into your own words, connect it with ideas you already have, how are you going to apply it?
