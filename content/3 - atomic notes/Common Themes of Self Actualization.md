# Common Themes of Self Actualization
Nietzsche's Will to Power
Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs
The Hero's Journey

All have in common the overcoming of a hardship / failure to reach a new transcended state. One characterized by the conquering of earlier hardships and a release of "weights" that weigh on us. Whether internal or external.

---
#### Reference
The overcoming of hardships as a vehicle to understanding highlights the [[importance of failure]]. In attaining that higher state, a trough had to be scaled before a peak. [[Valley of Despair and the Heroes Journey]].
