# The Human Condition is Social Condition

We are at our core social creatures. Everything must come from our ability to socialize. Our survival and thriving came from our ability to socialize. We couldn't not eat and reproduce if we could not socialize. We put too much emphasis on the end, the benefits that come from social interaction and not the means to the end, the social interaction itself.
