# Reaffirm Intellectual Ego

You can't possibly begin to imagine how much we as Humans feel the need to reaffirm our intellectual ego. Emergent complexity but turned inwards. Where our littlest actions are too reaffirm our intellect and rank in the social hierarchy.

Think about what you have to done to uphold the narrative you created about your intellect. How often you know you are wrong, but it would hurt your pride too much to say so. So you keep arguing moot points. A cancer that must be excised from you. Allowing your ego to lead your life is not necessarily bad, but allowing it to overrule your path self actualization is. 

To self-actualize, work on being consciousness of when your pride takes control. Pride is not a bad thing, but pride before reason is stupidity. Pride before logic is for animals. As the reasoning animal, you need to live up to the name. Allow yourself to be prideful, but don't allow yourself to be blinded.
