## Attempts to Prevent Communication Scramble

Communicating with humans is one big game of telephone, as our message is communicated to another person, that persons interpretation of the message change (even if ever so slightly) creating a cascade effect. 

Throughout history our efforts to stop this communication scramble has led us to creative solutions. Take religion. In hunter gatherer tribes, we needed a way to explain the world around us to every person in our group. We also needed a way to communicate our history and why we were here. So we essentially needed a [[collective consciousness]] that allowed everyone to be on the same page. Enter religion. Religion bridged the communication gap by: 1)creating a common origin 2) explaining why the world is the way it is and 3) how humans should operate in that world and what the goal was to move forward towards. Thus, a collective consciousness was born. Our own version of a hive mind that was able to propel us forward.

In our modern day, we use science and 18 years of education to try to create a baseline understanding of the world so that any new information can be built off of that baseline standing. The same as what hunter gatherers tried to do with religion, we try to do with education and science.
