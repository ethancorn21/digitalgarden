# Why I Like Talking to Nerds

All my life I have loved conversing with people who would be stereotypically considered nerds. Something about being in the presence of someone you know that isn't critiquing you, and even if they are, its a fucking nerd, who cares?
To broaden this. Why care what anyone says. Instead of saying "its a fucking nerd who cares", try to get the same affect with "its a fucking person who cares".
I can be someone who gets others to open up and be their true selves around. How? Well , I can't have any air of criticism about me. No person shall feel the need to put on any performance in my presence. They should feel that all I ask of them is themselves.
