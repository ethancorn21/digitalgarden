# Remedying the Inherent Problem with Communication
I figure that if we want to narrow the understanding gap, we must learn to use precise words in such a way that there is no mistaking your meaning for anything other than what you intend.

A most basic example: "Get my that green spoon with the swirly handle on the tan wooden table in the kitchen". A little specific no? perhaps, but when dealing with something whose nature can fall between your fingers like a handful of sand and pick up meaning, thoughts that matter need to be communicated directly and succinctly.
