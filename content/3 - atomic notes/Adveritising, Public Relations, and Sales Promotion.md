Planning and executing an ad campaign:
1) identify the target audience
	- market research
2) set advertising objectives
	- ad campaign / plan
	- informative ads
	- persuasive ads
	- reminder ads
	- Each ad can also have an ad focus
		- product focus 
		- institutional focus
		- public service focus
3) determine the ad budget
4) convey the message
	 - whats the message then what appeal to use
	- informational and emotional appeals
5) evaluate and select the media
	 - media buy
	 - mass media vs niche media
6) create ads
7) assess impact
	 - pretesting and posttesting



ad copy consists of:
1) headline
2) subhead
3) body copy
4) brand elements (logos etc.)
