[[MGMT3620 - Supply Chain Operations]]

First tier suppliers provide directly to a company for use in their final product. 

Second tier suppliers supply tier 1 suppliers, so they are one step removed from the final product.

### Ex:
	If a company were to supply metal to a car exhaust manufacturer, and then the car exhaust manufacturer were to provide their exhausts to a car company. 
	To the car company, the exhaust manufacturer is a tier 1 supplier and the metal supplier is tier 2.
