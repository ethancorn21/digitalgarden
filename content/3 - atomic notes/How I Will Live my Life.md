# How I Will Live my Life

In developing a vision for how I wish to live life, shall I go down the road of creating a vision for mind, body, spirit? Or shall I create a unifying principle that acknowledges and pertains to all three? Maybe I should focus on one singular thing at a time. Focus my attention whole heartedly on it. Maybe the better question is: "who do I wish to be?"

I want to help people. To be useful more like. I want to be surrounded by people that I help and maybe help me as well. To be good to others. Not in an altruistic way, but in a loyal way. I want to be capable. Ready to do what needs to be done physically, mentally, emotionally.

I guess my vision comes down to 2 things. Being capable. Being surrounded by needed by others. Lets define these 2 components.

By being capable, I can handle whatever is thrown at me. Physically capable. Intellectually sharp. Emotionally intelligent. These are broadly what will make me capable. The second component: being surrounded and needed by others. I guess this is a byproduct of not only being capable, but being relied upon by others. I want others to rely upon me. To feel comfortable doing so. To know that whenever they are dealing with me, it is a win-win situation. I want to be dependable and trust worthy. Someone to admire and inspire. 
 
From these guiding principles, a life of abundance.
