# Science, Education, Religion
Science and education is the new religion. Religion is theorized to have started as a way to create a baseline understanding within hunter gatherer groups to answer: how the world works, why we are here, what we should do. The goal of science and education is much the same. Science discovers new understanding how the world works. How we came to be. And how best to go about living our lives. Education fuels this by communicating that to people as baseline of understanding. This baseline allows people to learn new sciences and knowledge, as well as interact with others about that knowledge. Much the same as what early religion set out to do. 
What used to be shamans which performed rituals and ensured the cohesion of the group, now professors and teachers perform scientific rituals to ensure the cohesion of the group (society) through a universal understanding of how the world generally works.

Imagine if shamans did not teach their religion to their tribes, it would have quickly died out and with it the knowledge of how they believed the world worked along with it. Imagine if teachers did not teach their craft. It would quickly disappear and with it, the knowledge of how they believed it fit into wider society.
