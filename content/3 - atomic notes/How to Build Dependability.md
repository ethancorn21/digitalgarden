# How to Build Dependability

In order to be depended upon you must first be capable enough to be trusted. Trust stems from the perception that you can be relied upon. That what you say will happen. Start by not making false promises and dumb statements. Your word needs to become your bond. Don't openly speculate, yet don't quietly discover. When you reign in your word, your actions must follow. You have to be capable in whatever domain you find yourself in. Being the odd man out knowledge wise is never a good position to find yourself in. 

When you find yourself capable, you will also find people will need you. So, that is what makes up the second component. Being surrounded by people that depend on and need you. Be kind to people. Be interested in them for who they are, not what you wish them to be. Always offer to help others close to you, expecting nothing in return. Ask questions, be kind. Don't people please. Inspire and people will admire.

Dependability is a source of power that you must not abuse. A gift given to you non-consciously from a vulnerable place.
