# The Oracles
Oracles were granted the power of foresight, the ability to see the future and act as a conduit to the gods. Yet, they play a fractional part in mythology. Hermits that are barely even a side character in many tales. Making no lasting contributions that etched any of their names into eternity. No thanks, I'll stick with my hindsight.

Yes, being an oracles makes for a rather shitty story. A character to lead others to a path that you dare not venture.
