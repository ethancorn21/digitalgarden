# What most people call obsession, is really just effort

Everyone has become *fixated* on one thing, its automatic. It takes no effort to do. Its automatic because it feels good to fixate on it. In one way or another we get a reward from it. The vast majority of people will quit when their fixation is no longer enjoyable, but when you keep pushing past the pain of doing something unenjoyable, it starts to becomes an obsession. But not everyone has become obsessed. Obsession is painful and boring. Becoming obsessed requires you to act on your fixation even after the point it has become no longer enjoyable. Obsession is consistent effort.
