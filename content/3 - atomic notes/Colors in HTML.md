types of colors:
- named colors
	- style="color:darkseagreen;" 
- rgb colors
	- style = "background-color:rgb(143,188,143)"
- hexadecimal colors
	- style = "background-color: \#fbc8f;"
- Hue, Saturation, and Lightness (HSL) colors
	- style = "background-color: hsl(120,25%,65%);"
- RGBA (Red, Blue, Green Alpha (transparency / opacity))
	- style = "background-color: rgba(143,188,143,.5);"

Alpha is a value for RGBA and HSL, it simply refers to opacity
