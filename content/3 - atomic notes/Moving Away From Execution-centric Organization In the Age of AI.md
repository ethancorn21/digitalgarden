# Moving Away From Execution-centric Organization In the Age of AI

Organizations revolve themselves around execution. Delivery of a plan on the planned date, of the planned quality, using the planned people. The goal of execution-centric organizations is to maximize throughput of business operations by standardizing execution.

The problem is AI. How will it disrupt the organizational hierarchy. The goal of a manager used to be to guide the strategic vision. But now everyone will have set their sights on the strategic vision with a fleet of AI agents to build with. 

The strategy level with which organizations of old operate will be completely different. I figure we will most likely all most into architectural positions rather than IC hands on the keyboard work.
