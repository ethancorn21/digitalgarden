# The Stories We Make of Ourselves
The stories we make of who we are and what we stand for are often flat out wrong. We identify ourselves with ideals of who we are rather than the reality of what we are. We sell ourselves on identities that loosely fit ideals that we wish to embody. Often times, the stories and our true identity are complete opposites of who we truly are.
The dumbest person you know thinks himself the intellectual. The most irrational person thinks himself a logical person. A person who does nothing but bullshit thinks they keep it real with others. The liar does not realize he tells lies, but thinks of all times he tells the truth. Its the way of the world. 

So if we are going to live by ideals that we place for ourselves we might as well live as ideals that propel us forward. For example, do not think yourself the intellectual, think yourself the person that is in pursuit of realizing their intellectual potential. This turns you from someone who needs to defend their intellectual status to someone who is comfortable that they are still on their intellectual journey, which means its ok to be wrong / not know something. A simple reframe of perspective.
