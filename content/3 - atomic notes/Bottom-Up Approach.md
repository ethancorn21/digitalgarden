# bottom-up approach


In too many things we tend to look at the top idea and then draw conclusions to support that idea. We as humans are constantly searching for an end to a means, rather than a means to an end.

For example: When we write essays we begin with the topic we want to write about and then research to support that topic. When deciding to do anything in our lives we first recognize a want, then ways to get that want. We decide what we career we wish to do in life based on an outcome rather than what we are already good at and like doing.

What if instead, we figured out the foundation before the roof? We work bottom-up.
