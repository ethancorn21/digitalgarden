# meetings != productivity

Managers idea of productivity is meetings, because they are managing. But for the individual contributors, those meetings are killers of productivity because they are not contributing but informing. To increase productivity, ensure that individual contributors are maximally working with no distractions, while managers stay in meetings.

I think of each team as a LAN. The individual contributors are the end hosts and the managers should be the routers. The managers communicate with other managers and members under those managers through the manager. The members communicate to managers through a service that does not take their time like meetings and can be easily updated with progress checks and information.
