how can i marry systems with data? Every system operates off its own data, utilizing it. The network carries data. Servers and computers need their own data. But more granular, how do i marry systems by taking data from both systems, understanding how they interact, then create actionable insights off that data? At the most basic, dashboards. But go beyond the dashboards and montoring and create automation. This is where your automation comes in. Think in systems but dont lose sight of the details.
