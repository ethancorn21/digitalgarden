# In Pursuit of Intellectual Humility

To be Human is to be an egotistical creature. We evolved to be tribal creatures and our ego was our life force.

I need to be maximally in pursuit of intellectual humility. The only way that I can succumb to the knowledge that I need in order to achieve my goal's is to allow my mind to dilate past the control of my ego and just let myself intake and store the information without attachment from any personal bias or ego, that way when the information is disproven or no longer needed, I can simply let go of it. 

You must be able to say that you are wrong. That your ideas are probably not entirely correct. What you say will not be entirely what you meant. And this will be ok with you. It must be if you wish to dilate your mind.
