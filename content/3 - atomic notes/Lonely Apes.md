# Lonely Apes

We are but lonely apes. Hoping someone reaches their hands out, grabs us, and takes us with them. We only want to find our tribe, our belonging. I suspect it's a residue from a more primitive upbringing. To social creatures, to be tribe-less is to face certain death. Our intense fear of loneliness, FOMO, insecurities. They stem from our ancestors developing these psychological mechanisms to survive. To not be kicked out of the tribe. It's tribalistic ape shit all the way down as far as Humans are concerned.
