# Slow and Deliberate Speech

Speaking is an art form. You must take every opportunity to speak, conversate, articulate as practice for what you wish to convey. You are not simply yammering on about what you want to talk about, you have to include a point you are trying to get across. A reason for speaking. 

In the conveyance style, you must speak at the pace that you can efficiently pick thoughts and form them into coherent and deliberate sentences. Think before you speak and then think while you speak. This will probably require you to slow down and talk at a slower, more relaxed pace. This is fine. If what you are saying is thought over and not just you yammering, than that slowness is offset by the conciseness and precision of your words.
