# Free Will as the Unfolding of Causal Links
 The universe is a causal entity. A series of reactions that has taken place since the beginning of the universe. If everything is a series of reactions to the last "thing", can we not say that in this universe everything happens, BECAUSE of a reason. So, since everything happens because of a reason and nothing truly happens on its own, we can then also say there are a series of causal links that has happened since the beginning of time which we can trace back to see what has caused what. 

Then, would consciousness and free will also not be apart of the causal links? You might define free will as your ability to make choice. But are these choices not based off of what has happened in past causal links? If these decisions are past off of past causal links and these links are unfolding as you act throughout your life, has your decisions and actions not already been determined by these past causal links?
