# Process Strategy
aka. process selection

The way the production goods or services are organized

Types of Process strategy:
- Product focus (high volume, low variety)
- Process focus (low volume, high variety)
- Repetitive focus: high volume, high variety(assembly lines / batch production)
- Mass customization focus: mass production of high customizability
