# Purpose of Thoughts
We can crank out as many thoughts as we want, but whats the point if they are not leading to something grander than the might of our mind. A bricklayer does not lay the brick to say he laid the brick. He does it to erect a structure. A structure that takes it place among other structures to form towns and cities. With this in mind, do you wish to be the bricklayer who works with small forms of baked clay. Or do you wish to be the stone mason who works with massive blocks of dense stone to fulfill grand architectural plans that will stand for centuries as an ideal to strive for. a building that serves as a paragon of beauty and craftsmanship. Craftsmanship that other stonemasons strive for. Sure being the stonemason is harder, blocks are larger, weights are measured in tons per block. But your contribution is more substantive. Your result has more impact.

Our thoughts should be building our brain to an ideal that we strive toward. Like a self referential loop.
