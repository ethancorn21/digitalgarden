[[psychology]], [[synthesis]], [[Social Behavior]]; [[Evolutionary Psychology]]

Perhaps we value family and blood because it is evolutionarily beneficial for our genes. Evolution is about passing on genes, not individual survivability. Humans are social creatures as a mechanism of survival which allows us to pass on our genes, not necessarily our individual traits.
