# All you have in life is time and energy

Time and energy is all we have in life. Everything else that makes us who we are was created by aiming energy at it over a period of time. That's it.

Monetary status is built off of knowledge + time and energy. Knowledge is time and energy spent learning something. Essentially time + energy = our baseline value of anything. Think of anything that could possible be possessed by a person. It is a byproduct of time and energy.
But what about family / relationships? To foster deeper bonds and build our relationships does it not really only take a little bit of effort compounded over time? 
What about spirituality? Does piousness not come at the expense of consistent worship? For Christians, congregation at church every Sunday, and if you are particularly dutiful, prayer and reading the Bible every day. Is your faith not being strengthened by only your time and energy?
It's sort of like the atom. Everything is made up of the atom. Just as who we are, what we have, what we know. It is made up of our time and energy.
