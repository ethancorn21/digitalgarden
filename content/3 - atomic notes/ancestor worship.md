# ancestor worship
The worship of the spirit of deceased ancestors which is believed to influence the world of the living.
at ancient sites ([[Jericho]]), the skulls of ancestors were kept in the living rooms of houses and used every day as a portal to their believed spirit world. The holder of the skull will be given other worldly advice/secrets from the spirit of the deceased ancestor.
