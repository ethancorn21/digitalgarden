## nobody ever truly understands what we communicate

Thoughts are formless intangible concepts created by unknown mechanisms in the brain. Emotions, memories, desires, words, and pictures. Such deep abstractions happen in consciousness that we are like a virtualized intelligence running on top of organic hardware. Not fully formed until spoken into word. But by putting these thoughts into words, they lose the efficacy of what is *truly* meant. Imperfect words that do not cover the vast scope of thought are chosen as a tangible stand ins to represent them. Then these words are heard by another party who runs already misaligned words through their biases and own perceptions, deciphering whatever meaning they wish to gain from it. They then form a thought which again, is a big cloud of nothingness and everything. Misalign their thoughts into words again, you hear their words run them through your own biases and perception and *voila*. Has anybody truly begun to understand anybody?

Until a more efficient technology that allows us to bridge the translation layer and keep communication between people as thoughts, we will be stuck with inefficient communication.
