# Jesus the Apocalyptic Preacher

Jesus by historical account was an apocolyptic preacher. Not anything special by the day. He was a traveling apocolyptic preacher who also did exorcisms, nothing special for the day. He predicted that he might create his own political kingdom. Evidenced by him implying that he was the messiah, which at the time was seen more like a military figure than anything else. Also evidenced by when he tells the apostles that each will sit on a throne ruling the 12 tribes of israel. Did Jesus expect to die? maybe. Did he expect to be crucified? Probably not. Its why he says "why did you forsake me god" while dying on the cross. His followers could have reinterpreted his defeat as actually a win.
