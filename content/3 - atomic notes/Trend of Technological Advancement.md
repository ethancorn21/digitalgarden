# Trend of Technological Advancement
As technology moves forwards, it becomes smaller, easier to use, and harder to master.

Phones as a consequence have reached the maximum potential for this specific medium in which they embody. From landlines to wireless. From bricks to slabs. What will the new medium be? Probably electrical signals. Why interact with foreign hardware when our brains have the native domestic hardware? Why waste energy translating thoughts into physical motion of the fingers and hands when the maximally efficient thing to do is to simply think.
