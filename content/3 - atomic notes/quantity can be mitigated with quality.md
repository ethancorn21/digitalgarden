[[process]]

If quantity is ever needed, it can be mitigated with quality.

Quantity and quality are often seen as a pendulum. Go to one side and you must sacrifice the other. But if you go far enough to  quality, they can make up for the quantified need. 5 people to manage 500 things can be automated by 1 person paid 3x the salary. 150k salary will attract candidates 3x as good as the 75k salary position.
