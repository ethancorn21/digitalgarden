# Arbitrage
Greatest arbitrage opportunity is notice supply and demand discrepancies in real life. We are reaching a point where 90% of economic wealth is being held by 10% of people. So that in the future, no longer will the yeoman class be catered to anymore, only this 10% of people with the vast majority of the wealth will be catered towards.
For you, this means that majority of new tech services will be catered to F500 companies that have billions to blow on anything. This leaves an obvious supply discrepancy for the other 90% of the population who is holding 10% of the wealth. The opportunities will be more volumes by the sales will be lower. The question then is to decide a strategy. Fewer hits but those hits are big ticket winners. More hits but those hits are small.  The quality strategy will require that your product be exquisite.
