# Is Justice Institutionalized Revenge

Justice simply plays the part of outsourcing revenge. If humans were exact creatures that conducted themselves off of Newtons Laws naturally, equal but opposite reactions, then we would have no need for instutional justice. For ever wrong, we would wrong equally back. As such a balanced karmic debt would be paid and there would be no need for a third party as every punishment is equal. Of course we do not do this and it would open the possibility of a visous cycle of revenge like the old family blood feuds of old America. 
So we outsource our revenge to a justice system that we hope gives us our equal yet opposite revenge.
