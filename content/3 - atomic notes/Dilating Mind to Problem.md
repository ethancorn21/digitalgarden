# Dilating Mind to Problem

You must allow your mind to dilate to the problem at hand. It will intake the information needed to solve the problem, but you must feed it 1) quality enough information 2) enough time to form subconscious connections between patterns 3) remain concentrated on only a singular problem at a time. Remaining concentrated on a singular problem will be the hardest part. Your mind will want to drift in search of easier problems to solve. But you must reign it in.

You will know you are dilating your mind to fit the problem at hand when you begin to have dreams about said problem. Your subconscious is churning and computing solutions for you.
