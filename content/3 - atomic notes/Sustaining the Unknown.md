# Sustaining the Unknown
Your mastery of something will be determined by how long you can keep working on something to figure it out yourself. That unknown is grinding. With the ease of retrieval of information now, we have been conditioned to seek out or look up answers as soon as the first sticking point is hit. The average person will be in the unknown for 2 minutes on a problem before they seek help.

With this said, at what point will it be better to seek answers than work through it? Where is the diminishing rate of return where the energy expended into a problem is wasted because you miss a crucial piece of knowledge.
