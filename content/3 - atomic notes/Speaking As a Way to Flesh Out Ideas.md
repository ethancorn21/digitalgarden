# Speaking As a Way to Flesh Out Ideas

I cannot allow myself to live with half-baked ideas that I keep in my head and never truly flesh out and expand upon. Writing can get me partially the way there. Laying out my thoughts in a logical manner that allows my brain to rake and sift for what I concisely mean. 

But speaking is an entirely different medium. In order to explain something in the verbal manner, you are trying to explain what you would in writing but under a time crunch and a fight to pick the exact right words. 

As with anything, practice speaking out what you think. Write and speak. These should be hallmarks for any idea that you hold. There should be no half-baked idea that you subscribe to. This will allow you to reflexively explain ideas that you have to others and convey your thought process. You must become a good speaker.
