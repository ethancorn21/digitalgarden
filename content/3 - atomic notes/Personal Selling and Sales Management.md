[[marketing]]

personal selling - sales man talking to you essentially, has to include a back and forth

relationship selling - focused on long term relationship

personal selling process:
1) generate and qualify leads
2) pre-approach
	- research 
	- develop plan
	- rehearse
3) sales presenting and overcoming reservations
4) closing the sale
5) follow-up

independent agents - people who are contracted out by the company to sell their products
