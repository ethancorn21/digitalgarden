# Spend Time Deliberately Thinking

Spend more time deliberately thinking about a specific topic / problem. This can range anywhere from how to specifically deal with certain people to internal audits of why you think the way you do. 

I am creating a master list underneath the [[Things to Meditate On]] tag that has a complete list of topics to deliberately think about and give me insights when done. The cool things is I can continually iterate on my meditations by building off previous deliberate thinking sessions.
