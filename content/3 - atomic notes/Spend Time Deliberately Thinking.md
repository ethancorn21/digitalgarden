# Spend Time Deliberately Thinking

Spend more time deliberately thinking about a specific topic / problem. This can range anywhere from how to specifically deal with certain people to internal audits of why you think the way you do. Stare at a wall with a sticky note of the thing you wish to think about. Don't allow yourself to leave your position. You will reach a meditative state around the 15 - 20 minute mark.

I am creating a master list underneath the [[things to contemplate]] tag that has a complete list of topics to deliberately think about and give me insights when done. The cool things is I can continually iterate on my meditations by building off previous deliberate thinking sessions.
