# Thoughts as an Indicator of Brain Function

Thoughts and great ideas are indicators of our mind. Thoughts and ideas are the most tangible thing that we can produce in our head. Like examining the craftsmanship of a article of clothing to determine the overall state of a factory. Our brain can be examined much the same. Our thoughts, whether weighty and full of quality, or non-substantive, are a direct reflection of the mental processes which created them. Inputs, Connection, storage, retrieval.
