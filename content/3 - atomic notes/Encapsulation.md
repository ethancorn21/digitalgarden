Encapsulation is the basic idea that no outside code should ever modify your internal values inside of classes. There should be checks (categories) so that fields(internal variables) are not changed to something that makes no sense.
For example, if you were interacting with a banking app, if the app did not follow proper encapsulation, you could just set MyAccount.balance(1,000,000).
