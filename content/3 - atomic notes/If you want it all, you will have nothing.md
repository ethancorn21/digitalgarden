# If you want it all, you will have nothing
Everything has a price. You have to pay that price enough times to reach a critical mass to succeed. If you try to pay that price for success in everything, you will never reach enough critical mass in one thing to be successful. What are you willing to sacrifice for that? You have to be willing to sacrifice anything that is not that.
