# peasant mindset and the bicameral mind

With the breakdown of the bicameral mind, the voices of the "gods" disappeared. In came our own voice, where we began to create the concept of the analog "I". 

Is this why we are always looking for the next thing to rule our lives and dedicate ourselves to? In the absence of a god-like voice that commands us, do we fill the primal urge for that voice through chasing meaning or things that are supposed to give us meaning or fulfillment?
