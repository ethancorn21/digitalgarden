# Unconscious Processing

We get "aha!" moments seemingly out of nowhere. When brushing our teeth, showering, sleeping, etc. any idle activities. Allowing your mind to idle and wander on its own is great because it allows your non conscious mind to compute things that you are not aware of. 

Putting your thoughts and notes down into an external environment allows the brain to move onto the next thing with out wasting mental bandwidth on holding the information in the working memory.
