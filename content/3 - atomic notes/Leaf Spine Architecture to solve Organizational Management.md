# Leaf Spine Architecture to solve Organizational Management

How to stop the endless drudgery of meetings?
When you think of how organizations operate, it is great for top down but not east-west like 3 tier network architecture is. Workers now need to talk to an increasing amount of disconnected workers. Meetings are not the answer to this. Can we integrate a new system that acts as the SPINE part of the business? Each LEAF connects to each SPINE and each SPINE connects to each LEAF. That way, east-west chatter across orgs is now efficient and the least amount of time bandwidth is taken by an expected ratio of meeting times.
