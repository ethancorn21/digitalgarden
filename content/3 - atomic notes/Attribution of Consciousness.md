# Attribution of Consciousness

We attribute consciousness to things to things that we don't know if they are conscious. We feel bad for the fly we just swatted because we feel empathy because we can understand that we ourselves do not wish to be swatted like that. We talk to dogs as if we know they can understand us. We say they have personalities. This of course is dependent on the discussion, what is consciousness?
