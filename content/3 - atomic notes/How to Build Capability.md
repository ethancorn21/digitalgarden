# How to Build Capability 

Capability consists of:
1) Physically capable
		Take care of your health. Do not allow yourself to waste away. But also don't stress out if you are not the most physically capable. That's not the goal here. The goal is to be good enough in this domain so that you are never hindered or set back because of your physicality. So, go to the gym a few times a week. Get good sleep. Eat well. Get some sun. That's it.
2) Intellectually sharp
		You can't be a dullard. You need good mental models as well as a plethora of information with which to draw from. This is important because you need to be able to think the right thoughts at the right time in order to be who you wish to be. So, read a lot. Store, think, and implement information you obtain. Do not be afraid to show your expertise to anyone. It's ok to be intelligent, fuck em if they hate you for it.
3) Emotionally Intelligent
		This is an internal battle that will always be fought. You must not allow yourself to be a slave to your emotions. You choose how you wish to live life, not your emotions. So, practice knowing what you are feeling, why you are feeling, what your emotions are trying to get you to do, then practice living with them. Don't distract yourself from them. Don't run away from them. Live with them. Experience them in their fullest.
