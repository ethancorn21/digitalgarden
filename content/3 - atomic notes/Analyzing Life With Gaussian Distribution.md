# Analyzing Life With Gaussian Distribution
How can I begin to use Gaussian Distribution to analyze probability / data in my life?

I must think of data points in my life by thinking of the mean. The majority of data points will exist at or near the median. The life experiences will mostly be average excitability. But as I add more experiences, I will experience a greater quantity of higher quality experiences.

I must begin to expect new experiences to be "average" but recognize that for grand life altering experiences, I must put myself out there enough to overcome statistical probability of my experience staying with 2-3 standard deviations of the mean experience.
