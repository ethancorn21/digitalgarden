# Severing the Corpus Callosum and System 1
In an experiment where the Corpus callosum was severed, subjects created reasons for doing things that were different then the instructions given. Similar to the System 1 causal inference of WYSIATI of Kahneman, the brain leaps to conclusions to have a unified story, even if that story does not make much sense.

Takeaway : We strive for understanding rather than truth in understanding
