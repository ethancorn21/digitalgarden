[[value creation]], [[synthesis]], [[analysis]]

---
The amount of value you bring to the table is how much say you definitively have in the world.

Before the red pill allegations start, my value is my impact on my environment. If I wish to change the world for the better, to be useful, to create things that genuinely enrich people's lives in ways they themselves cannot quite comprehend. I must drastically increase my value.

I do not define my value as a tangible dollar amount. Instead I see it as my potential to influence variables: family, friends, and the world around me. This is my value. From this, my value gives me the power and control I need to help others.
