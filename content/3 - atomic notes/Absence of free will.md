# Absence of free will

because of the way we learn, we draw from the past to react to the present. With a similar mechanism, we also try to predict the future from events of the past. So our present and future decisions are made based off of a fixed past. Would it not be reasonable to say that our decisions are also fixed since the time we have experienced the past that would eventually lead to our present decisions? 

So then are we just reactive beings, locked into how we will act because the past?
