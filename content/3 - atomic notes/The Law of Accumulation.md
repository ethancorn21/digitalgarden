# The Law of Accumulation

Little things add up to create big things. Compounding allows for small wins to build off of each other, multiplying off of each other and increasing their effect. 

little wins build off of each other creating exponential growth.

### Example:
When people think of "overnight successes" they do not see the massive amount of small things that eventually lead to their success.

In the stock market, money consistently invested over years compounds and grows exponentially. Little increments of reinvested dividends or stock growth amount to earning more than if that money had sat in cash.
