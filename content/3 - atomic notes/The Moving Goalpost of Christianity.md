# The Moving Goalpost of Christianity

Christianity has built an entirely unwinnable debate simply by the phrase, "God works in mysterious ways". Now anything can be explained away as, part of the design. God used to create the lightning, now God created physics. An unwinnable argument by both sides and yet nothing is accomplished. An indestructible defense.

Theological debates will always evolve. Religious texts will be interpreted in different ways and fall on differing ears. The goal is to not get drawn into a protracted debate about religion. Its an area full of landmines on both sides. Acknowledge the beauty and usefulness of the doctrines and how they can bring a society together behind a unified belief. But nothing more. You won't change anyones mind and that is cool with me.
