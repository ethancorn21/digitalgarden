# Intellectual Ego

Be careful hurting people’s intellectual ego. It’s one of the things they can’t let slide. Most people anyways. These people will often show themselves in little ways during your interactions. Say you are working on something and rephrase what they said to gauge if you understood what they meant. If they said something defensive like, “that’s what i said”, or “yeah that’s literally what i was talking about”. They feel the need to have their ideas contributed to them. They have to have others know that they idea floating around the psycho sphere is theirs. No problem, let it be theirs. You can't hold any satisfaction in others thinking you are smart.
