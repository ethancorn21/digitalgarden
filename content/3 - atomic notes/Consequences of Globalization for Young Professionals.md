# Consequences of Globalization for Young Professionals

Jobs are exponentially harder to land and retain than they were 10,20,30 years ago. Globalization means that global resources are no longer confined by geographic boundaries.

Things impacting todays job seekers:
- hyper competitive work environment
	- economical downturn means that there are more workers than jobs available
	- connectivity with high speed internet connections means that jobs can be outsourced for a fraction of the cost
- AI boom
	- companies are FOMO'ing into heavy investments in AI replacement rather than AI leverage
- Change in corporate culture
	- the days where companies valued their veterans who stayed with the company for decades are gone. Measurable impact is the only metric with which to keep people now.
