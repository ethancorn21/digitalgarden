# settling the frontier and human advancement
Settling the frontier is much like the advancement of the human race. We have a frontier (new sciences and discoveries) that are eventually explored, charted, mapped which leads to more people flooding into those frontiers to now become apart of the homogenous mix. The frontier then moves further outwards, away from the homogeneous mix. It is explored, charted, mapped, repeat to infinity.
