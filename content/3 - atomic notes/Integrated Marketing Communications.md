if org influences someones actions to do / buy something it is probably because of the promotion aspect of the 4 P's

Communication Process to communicate with consumers:
1) The sender
2) the transmitter
	- who the sender works with to develop marketing comms, example: the advertising department / agency
3) encoding
	- turning the sender's idea into a tangible message
4) communications channel
	- the medium that carries the message
5) the receiver
	- who reads, sees, hears the message
6) noise
	- any interference that gets in the way of the intended uptake of the message
7) feedback loop

### When consumers receive the message:

AIDA Model
- Aware (think)
- Interest (feel)
- Desire (feel)
- Action (do)

Awareness
- Brand awareness
	- how well a customer can remember a brand's name and products / services
- Aided recall
	- The name is known when a brand is presented to them
- Top-of-Mind awareness
	- "whats a type of soda?"
	- "coke"
