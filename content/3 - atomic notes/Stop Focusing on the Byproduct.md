# Stop Focusing on the Byproduct

So often do we focus on the byproduct of something rather than the process that get's there.

Instead of idolizing money and doing things to get that money, realize that money is a byproduct of 1) skills and 2) knowledge, both of which are byproducts of[[All You Have In Life is Time and Energy| time and energy]]. If your goal was to produce as many Blueberry Munchkin Dolls as you can, are you going to focus on the Blue Berry Munchkin Dolls themselves or are you going to optimize your factory with the highest leverage techniques and equipment possible so that you can crank out these fucking dolls?
