# The Deep Work Hypothesis
"The ability to perform deep work is becoming increasingly *rare* at exactly the same time is is becoming increasingly *valuable* in our economy. As a consequence, the few who cultivate this skill, and then make it the core of their working life will thrive." - Cal Newport

The ability to concentrate deeply to learn and quickly master new skills is becoming rare. With the triumph of social media over the technological landscape, attention spans are narrowing and focus is becoming ever fragmented. The ability to hone in on one thing and devote your entire being to learning it will be the ultimate deciding factor in what separates those who are good from those who are the greats.
