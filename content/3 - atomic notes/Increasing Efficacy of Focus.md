# Increasing Efficacy of Focus

There are two mechanisms of action for what translate focus into momentum. Weight of focus and area over which your focus is dispersed. If you think of it as a weights and force. What would exert more force onto an object. 1 pound focused over 1 square inch = 12 pounds over a square foot. Reduce focus area and increase intensity = more efficacious focus. 

So simply. Meticulously cut out that which does not serve your highest goal.
