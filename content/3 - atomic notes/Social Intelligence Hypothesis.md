# Social Intelligence Hypothesis (aka Social Brain Hypothesis)

Social Intelligence Hypothesis posits that human intelligence and consciousness emerged because of our complex social structure.

Navigating a social group requires immense precision. You must know how is close to who, who hates who, who owes what, who to go to for specific problems. This juggling of the social relationships of an entire tribe of 30+ people would force the brain into a higher state of function if it where to survive.
