# Life Pours From You or On You

A life well lived is a consequence of what you do while living. If you wish to die on your deathbed with no regrets you must put yourself in positions where you can't give yourself the option to regret. When faced with the choice of the uncomfortable or the comfortable, why not choose the uncomfortable? Really, what do you have to lose but self-satisfaction of serving the hedonistic mind?
