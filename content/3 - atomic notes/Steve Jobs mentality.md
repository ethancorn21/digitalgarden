# Steve Jobs mentality

Steve Jobs was so unhappy with 99% of what he saw. He saw the disfunction in everything. Wasn't satisfied with anything. By all means a miserable way to live life. Yet, we revere him as a genius. A world changer. I never did before, but I get it now. 

In the pursuit of excellence you can't only crave it. You have to imbue it in everything you desire and bitch and nag your way towards it. You have to be destructively in pursuit of your goals. To what extent? I could only hope to keep the destruction and disfunction to my internal parts, will they bleed out into the world around me? probably.
