The ability to enter flow state is a non-tangible highly commoditized skill. The more I can enter and maintain flow state while working will slowly but incrementally increase the efficacy the work has on my skills and ability to do deep work. Essentially the more I enter flow, the better I get at maintaining and entering flow. It is a self-fulfilling loop.

To increase the efficacy and efficiency of your work, focus on flow state.
