[[process]] ; [[value creation]] ; [[Inversion Philosophy]]


The goal is to have a supreme focus on value provided. This means emphasizing the quality of the few over the quantity of the many. Allowing opportunity for degradation of quality, ripples down from bottom to top. We only have to look at the companies of the last decade to see how giants have fallen. Look at Apple or Google. People who provided themselves on simplicity and let their value speak for themselves have fallen. Look at the rise of the supergiants, all quite simple and intuitive to use. Use speaks for itself, not aesthetics. Though aesthetics do play into it, like Apple for instance.
