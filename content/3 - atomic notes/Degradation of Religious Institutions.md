[[religion]], [[Co-Evolution of Religion and Humans]]

Religions and humans grow side by side. Throughout history we have largely  been animist to abrahamic.  Religion has largely been there as we have created huge technological and sociological advancements. What happens then when religion, which has co-existed with us throughout the time, when we begin to explain away what was once unexplainable.

Was it created for us to explain away the unknown? Was this inevitable? Was our human desire to not be in the dark about the world around us, create an unavoidable chain to where we are now?
