# Intrigue of Parallel Worlds

We have always been drawn to the intrigue of alternate realities. Ones where we have superpowers, or there exists mythical, even extremely advanced technology. Myths and stories are fundamental to what it means to be a Human because escaping this reality just feels so good.

Drugs act as the same type of catalyst. An escape from this reality. A dulling of the pain in the back of your mind that signifies you are living. 

Is it any surprise then that we love our devices? Mental stimulation, which we crave, with no energy expenditure, which we instinctually try to conserve. The myths and stories that allowed us to live outside ourselves, are now contained within a single swipe.
