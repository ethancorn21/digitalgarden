# Big Stick Ideology

To cooperate with someone while implying the use of violence if cooperative demands are not met.
On the surface these talks where the big stick ideology is employed can seem peaceful and jovial, but underneath a one sided deal is being struck because one side is under the pressure of potential violence.

named after Theodore Roosevelt's famous saying, "speak softly but carry a big stick". Be cooperative, but show the violence you are capable of, so that the other party goes along with you.
