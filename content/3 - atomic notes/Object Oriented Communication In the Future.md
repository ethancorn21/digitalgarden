## Object Oriented Communication In the Future

To convey a thought, an idea must be formed from known words and known ideas. An imprecise process that never fully grasps the depth of each individual thought. The problem comes from translating two mediums that are not the same. Thoughts and mind matter into words. 

The future will allow us to send thoughts the same way we call objects and classes in Object Oriented Programming. Entire ideas and properties of those ideas will be transported at will, without room for listener misinterpretation. The efficacy of sending entire knowledge bases with a single communication, allowing parts of that knowledge base to be called on for any function, the same as we call object.methods().
