# Parkinson's Law
Work will expand to fit the time allotted to it.

This means that an opposite can be deduced from this. The work contracts to fit the time allotted to it.
### example:
if you assign 2 hours to do one task, it will take you 2 hours to do that one task. Give yourself 30 minutes, and you might not be able to finish all of the task, but you make dramatically more progress than you would have otherwise.
