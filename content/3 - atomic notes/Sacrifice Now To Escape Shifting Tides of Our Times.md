# Sacrifice Now To Escape Shifting Tides of Our Times

You have to sacrifice now if you wish to social upheaval that will follow our times. Artificial Intelligence changes the game. You must learn how to efficiently harness, direct, and multiple your efficiency through them. The skills that will run the world are not simply being a subject matter expert, but extrapolating your knowledge into dozens of agents who you must goad to do your bidding. 

The tides of society are shifting fast, what worked 5 years ago will not work today. You can't allow yourself to follow the traditional guidelines for what worked. Look around, and use that as a litmus test for what average is. It is shockingly low. There are people who refuse to use AI to complement their work. There are others who rely to wholly on it. You must find yourself in the middle. A force multiplier, but not a crutch.
