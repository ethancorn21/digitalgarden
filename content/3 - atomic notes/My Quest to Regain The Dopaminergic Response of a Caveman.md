# My Quest to Regain The Dopaminergic Response of a Caveman

If one wants to control themselves in an era of gluttonous opulence, the dopaminergic drive must be throttled to the max. Being in control of your dopaminergic drive gives you the ability to delay quick gratification that is within arms reach in order to work towards the slow gratification that is a mile away.

I have been substance and social media free for close to a year now. I used to feel that I was missing parts of the world. But by disconnecting from it all, I have a new found appreciation for the new monk like existence I have forged for myself.
