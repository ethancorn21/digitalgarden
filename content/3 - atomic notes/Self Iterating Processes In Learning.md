You must constantly refine and iterate how you learn. Knowledge work and the ability to make shit happen are the prime movers of value in the modern day. You must have the know how and the discipline to take your time and energy and focus it as a laser on what needs done. The things that bring value to yourself. You cannot sit idly by and be ok with skirting by. You have to take initiative and own it. It is you and only you that can implement processes to make yourself better everyday. Iteration is key. 

The question then moves to: "How can I implement self-iterating processes into my life". Do I know any self-iterating processes? One that immediately jumps out to me is writing, specifically reflecting and expounding. So I must implement more reflection and expounding of topics I am learning and things I am thinking of. Write. It's that simple. When you learn, write about it. When you reflect, write about it. You have to take those abstract thoughts that are made of incomprehensible brain actions and turn them into words. It forces you to make sense of what you are thinking and allows it to be audited. You can't even begin to audit a thought that hasn't been brought into the light.
