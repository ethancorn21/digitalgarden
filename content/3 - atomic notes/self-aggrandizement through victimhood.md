# Self-Aggrandizement through victimhood

self victimizing is a powerful way to remove agency from yourself. Being a victim to circumstances rather than your own actions that lead you to those circumstances is a rather dangerous game. 

You must only allow yourself one reaction to any circumstance in which you find yourself: "What has happened in the past happened, but what happens in my future is up to me".

I have known far too many people who have painted themselves out to be a victim in some way. Perhaps they didn't grow up in the perfect circumstances or maybe they decided that their future is fruitless so the present should be spent purely in enjoyment and not mitigating that terrible future they foresee. Perhaps it is a byproduct of the culture we live in. Self-aggrandizement through victimhood by any means necessary. Victimizing oneself can feel powerful in a way. Allowing the heavy burden that is living be passed from the shoulders of the self, to the shoulder of the external situation at hand.
