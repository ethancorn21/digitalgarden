# Newton on Intellectual Humility

>"If I have seen further it is by standing on the shoulder of Giants"
>- Newton in a letter to Robert Hooke

Intellectual humility. One of the greatest minds that has ever existed, which mined countless amount of novel knowledge, who opened our path to the stars attributes his success to those that came before him. Those that laid the path and sacrificed so that he may follow them and build upon their studies.

How easy would it have been for Newton to say, "suck deez, I'm the best". Of course, did he probably say this in his head? Of course, but outwardly to the world, he projected humility and that is how he will be known for millenia.
