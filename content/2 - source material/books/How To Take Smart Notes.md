### Summary:
The Zettalkasten system is a way of taking and storing notes such that over time, these notes become idea generation machines. The structure of the system allows hidden connections to take root, creating a complex weave of knowledge that builds off of each other. This interconnected building of knowledge not only allows you to declutter your mind, generate new insights, but it also increases the retention of new knowledge by connecting it to things you already know. This system of notes is especially useful for writers, academics, and note takers of any kind.
