Fathers of quality management
- W. Edwards Denning - Worked in Japan post WW2
	- 14 points of management
- Joseph M. Juran
	- Juran trilogy - three critical components of quality management
		- quality planning
		- quality improvement
		- quality control
	- Introduced pareto principle to supply chain (80/20 rule)
- Walter Shewart - Shewart chart aka control charts
	- monitors process stability over time
- Taiichi Ohno - Just in-time, Kanban system, Toyota Production System Dev
	- TPS Development - use JIT and automation to produce only what is needed when its needed
