### Jiang Xueqin Civilization Lectures

### summary: 
Religion allowed the birth of society. [[Religion is a collective consciousness]]. When we gathered in groups, that togetherness inspired a collective way of thinking. Which inspired more togetherness, which inspired more collective ways of thinking. A cycle. Hunter gatherers beliefs revolved around this cycle. Life proceeds death, yet also comes after it. Souls are reborn. Religion is about constructing an understanding of the world. Their understanding, their religion was their way of doing science. Religion was born when humans were hunter gatherers, society was born as a celebration of the hunter gatherer religions (see previous lecture for why).
