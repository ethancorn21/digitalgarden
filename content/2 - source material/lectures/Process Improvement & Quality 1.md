### Total Cost of Quality
- prevention costs - cost of prevents defects before they occur
- appraisal cost - cost of assessing quality level
- internal failure cost - defect cost before customer gets it
- external failure cost -  defect cost after customers gets it

### Yue - only good way to spend money is prevention cost, not reduce cost.
- because investment in prevention stops the others from occurring
- the goal is 0% defects, that way money is only spent on prevemtion.

### Total Quality Management
- managing every step of production to customer so that each is managed so each dimension that a customer might care about is maxed out.
- Customer focus
- leadership must emphasize quality
- always room for improvement
-  How to implement myself? [[Cultivating a cultural mindset for process improvement]]

#### continuous improvement
- sequence for problem solving and continuous improves
	- plan - what are some problems? How to fix / improve?
	- do - How to implement plan?
	- check - How are the results? Keep monitoring it
	- act - course correct where the implementation potentially messed things up.

#### empower employees to fix issues
- This is why small startups are able to accomplish big things in such little time.
	- but with time, orgs expand and bureaucracies set in.
- hire the best, pay them the best, give them the tools to fix problems. 
	- The best are the best for a reason. Leave them alone. Drop them into the warzone and back away until they ask for backup. 
	- Cost savings should not come from hiring budget. Empower those who want it.º
