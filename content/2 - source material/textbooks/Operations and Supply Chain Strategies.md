### Elements of a business
- structural elements
	- tangible resources like:
		- buildings
		- equipment
		- computers
	- require large capital investments
	- inflexible and mostly irreversible
- infrastructural elements
	- intangible resources:
		- people
		- policies
		- decision rules
		- organizational structure

---
### Strategy

Strategy is just a long term game plan
- long term usually mean several years
- the business's strategy comes from it's mission statement, its reason for existing
- then different business units like operations, IT, marketing, engineering, etc. use the business strategy as a guide for their own functional strategy
	- this ensure that each business unit is operating towards the businesses goal while having separate daily operations

For Ops and Supply Chain, a business strategy must define:
- who the firms targeted customers are and what systems need to be put in place to provide value to those customers
- Time frames and performance objectives
- identify and exploit organizational supply chain strengths (called "Core competencies")
	- core competencies are strengths developed that help sustain a competitive advantage
		- difficulty to copy by competitors
		- customers find value in them
