[[macroeconomics]]


[[Key functions of money]]:
Medium of exchange
unit of account
store of value

Commodity money = intrinsic value, gold silver
FIAT money = money we all agree on

money supply = checking's accounts + currency in circulation

M1 money (highest liquidity) = money in circulation, in checkings accounts, in travelers checls
M2 money (near money = M1 + money in savings accounts, investment account, time deposits (CDs)

stock = equity in a company
bonds (AKA securities) = IOU, you are the lender
Interest rates and bond prices are inversely related. High interest rates = low bond prices, you want high interest rates. Low interest rates = high bond prices, you want bonds.
