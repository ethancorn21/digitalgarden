new products important because longer market has been around = more saturation

[[diffusion of innovation]] - new innovations will spread through a market over time

adoption by bell curve:
innovators - 2.5%
early adopters - 16.5%
early majority  - 34%
late majority - 34 %
laggards - 16% of market

innovators - those who are the first to adopt
early adopters - those who adopt a new innovation after the innovators, usually first 34%. CRUCIAL TO PROFITABILITY
early majority - those who are third to adopt, this is usually when a product becomes profitable.
late majority - when these guys adopt, market reaches full market potential, slow saturation starts
laggards - never change, use till breaks, good for subscription products, terrible to sell new products to.

relative advantage - helps speed along diffusion process because it means your product is better in some way than others.
