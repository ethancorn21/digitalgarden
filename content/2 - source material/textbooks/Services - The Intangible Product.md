Customer service - helps marketers by helping them meet the needs and wants of customers

what differentiates services from goods?
Perishability - service can't be stockpiled
intangibility - it is not a real thing
Heterogenous - service differs from client to client
inseparability - consumption and production happens at the exact same time.

4 P's of marketing plus 3 P's of customer service:
Presentation
Personnel
Process

Service Gaps Model
designed to figure out where inefficiencies lie in a service provider so they can improve it.
consists of:
knowledge gap - difference in expectations
standards gap - standards of work ; expected by perceived
delivery gap - difference between the work standard and the quality of service provided
communications gap - real vs claimed communication ability

Customers usually use 5 perception of quality to decide if a service met their expectations:
1) tangibles - how good the things you can see / feel
2) empathy  - how help to ever single customer
3) assurance - helpfulness of employees
4) responsiveness - willingness to help
5) reliability - accuracy of service

service recovery - when delivery of service not good, can listen to customer complaint and feedback and compensate them.
