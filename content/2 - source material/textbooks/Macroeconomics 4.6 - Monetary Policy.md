[[macroeconomics]]


monetary policy is controlling the money supply to govern interest rates which governs spending and inflation.
money supply can be changed with:
1) money supply
2) discount rate
3) Open Market Operation - buys or sells government bonds from banks

increase money supply - buy bonds
decrease money supply - sell bonds
