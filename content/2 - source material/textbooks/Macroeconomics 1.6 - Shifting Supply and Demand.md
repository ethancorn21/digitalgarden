Increase or decrease in demand or supply, what shifter it is, what happens to price and quantity

1) Technology shifter, supply increases, price goes down quantity goes up
2) Price of related goods, demand increases, price goes up quantity goes up
3) not shifted, no change in supply or demand. price goes does Quantity goes up.
4) Supply goes down, Resource cost shifter. Price goes up, quantity goes down
5) Demand decreases, Expectation or taste and preference shifter. Price goes down quantity demanded goes down.
