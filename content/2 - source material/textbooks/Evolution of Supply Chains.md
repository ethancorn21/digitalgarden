[[MGMT3620 - Supply Chain Operations]]

Supply Chains are about moving physical products or services from their point of origin to the point of consumption.
The simplest supply chain comprises of products flowing downstream , from the manufacturer to the consumer.

---
Information in a supply chain are things like orders for more products
Supply chains consist of :
- source supplier
- manufacturer
- distributor
- retailer
- customers
---
Vertical Integration:
- companies try to be largely self sufficient in their supply chains
	- manufacturers not only source supplies but also distribute it to retailers as well
	- companies own the supply chain end to end
	- think about what Musk is trying to do with SpaceX
---
Supply chain evolved from local to global
- mid 20th century, resources were sourced near to where they were manufactured
- now, largely a global supply chain 
	- resources produced in one half of the world can be shipped to another half to produce a good
	- supply chains are more specialized in their part
	- primary goal: maintain a constant flow of products, information, and funds
		- SCM is about how to move products, information, and funds between systems and people to place it where it is most effective
