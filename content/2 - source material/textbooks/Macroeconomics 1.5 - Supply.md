The Law of Supply - direct relationship between price and quantity supplied
When price goes up, quantity supplied goes up as well

5 Shifters of Supply
1) Price of resources
	- change in price of inputs
2) Number of Producers
3) technology
4) taxes & subsidies
5) expectations

Price DOES NOT change supply

Change in price = move along the supply curve, change in quantity supplied
 Supply only changed with 5 Shifters

Market equilibrium = where quantity demanded and quantity supplied meet

Surplus = quantity supplied more than quantity demanded
shortage = quantity demanded more than quantity supplied
