[[macroeconomics]]


Unemployment Rate = (# unemployed / # in labor force) * 100 

Only includes people actively looking for jobs

Working age population = total population that is over 16 and under retirement age

Frictional unemployment - when people are switching or looking for jobs, temporary
Structural unemployment - Workers don't have the right skills, longer term
Cyclical Unemployment - workers losing jobs due to recession

Frictional and Structural unemployment = Natural Unemployment rate, will always be there
