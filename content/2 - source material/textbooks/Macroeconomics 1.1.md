
Scarcity - we have unlimited wants and needs but limited resources to fulfill those wants; need to choose between wants and needs. 
Resources (factors of production) = land, labor, capital, entrepreneurship
resources used to produce one thing can't be used to produce another, scarcity.
We look for the most efficient way to use our limited resources.
