Production possibility curve - shows tradeoffs, what can be produced with what amount of resources

[[Opportunity cost]] - specific number of things you give up to produce another thing.

![[Screenshot 2025-10-16 at 8.42.11 PM.png|200]]

To produce 1 Triangle it cost 3 squares. Each 1 square cost 1/3 triangles. This is constant opportunity cost.

Increasing Opportunity Cost
Law of Increasing Opportunity Cost
As you try to produce more of one thing, it gets harder to produce another. Opportunity cost increases more and more with each increase in quanity.

![[Screenshot 2025-10-16 at 8.46.41 PM.png|200]]

Points on the curve are inefficient, under inefficient and above impossible / unobtainable

PPF curve pushed outward by better or more resources. Better educated workers, better tech, etc. Pushes PCC outwards.
