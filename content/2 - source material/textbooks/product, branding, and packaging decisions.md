[[marketing]]
product = anything of value to a customer that can be bought or sold voluntarily

core customer value - what problem the product is fixing for the customer
actual product - the core customer value into an actual product with design, quality, etc.
associated services - the niceties that come along with the actual product like warranties.

Types of products:
Consumer products - things that consumers use for personal use
specialty / service products - these are high in customer loyalty, customers will go out of their way to get these

shopping products / services - products with a decent degree of risk, customers will spend a while trying things out, comparing, researching these types of products

[[convenience products / services]] - no thought products. Bread, soap, flour

[[unsought products / services]] - product that people don't know about, they don't recognize the problem that the problem is fixing.

product mix - all of the things offered by an organization. Probably made up of many product lines

product mix depth - how many things a product line includes
product mix breadth - how many product lines a product mix is made up of

product line depth - number of variations available for a product
product line breadth - number of different product lines a company creates

too much product breadth = [[cannibalize]]

[[brand equity]] - how valuable a brand is
made up of:
1) brand awareness
2) perceived value
3) brand associations
4) brand loyalty

Brand ownership usually by manufacturers / national brands or retailers / store brand
think Kirkland vs nationally recognized brand or Walmart's great value vs Nestle
