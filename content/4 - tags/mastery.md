# mastery
what does mastery look like?
effortless?
efficient?
an ability to adapt from a grand scale down to minute details?

Mastery is effortless efficiency. It sounds simple when distilled down. But, imagine being able to learn something that would take you hours to learn, in seconds because you have a foundational level of knowledge that allows you to extrapolate and infer details. Imagine being so knowledgeable that you can adapt to new knowledge rather than be bogged down by what you already know. 
Mastery is achieved through obsession over years. The majority of people will live their lives not mastering a single thing. Mastery takes grit to persevere the unenjoyable for years, to arrive at the paradise of being informed. A perspective that nobody else quite sees or understands. 
To master something, you have to be a little insane and strictly disciplined.
